Privacy Policy

Last update: 1.1.2026

1. Location (GPS)

The application accesses the location of the device for the purposes of automatically loading the traffic regulations of the given country, displaying the position of the sun/satellites and for the function of saving parking spaces in the Travel Diary and the "Where I park" module. These data are processed exclusively locally in the device and are not sent to any external servers.

2. Camera and AR technology

Access to the camera is used for augmented reality functions (AR Leveling, AR Spot Planner, Sun Path and Satellites). The camera image only serves for real-time visualization, is not recorded or sent anywhere.

3. Motion sensors (Accelerometer and Gyroscope)

The application uses motion sensors to measure the tilt of the caravan and determine the direction to the satellites/sun. These data are processed in real time in order to calculate the level and orientation in space.

4. Storage and photos

The application requires access to storage for saving photos and text records within the Travel Diary, as well as for saving vehicle settings and energy balance calculations. These files are stored in the application's internal memory. The user has full control over their deletion or voluntary sharing on social networks (via the system interface).

5. Purchase processing

All financial transactions are processed through the Google Play Billing and Apple App Store systems. The developer does not have access to your credit card data or bank information.

6. Third party links

The application provides links to official portals for the purchase of highway vignettes, or our partners' websites. These websites are operated by third parties and are governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Local data processing

All calculations (load distribution, payload, energy self-sufficiency) take place exclusively on your device. No entered parameters about your vehicle are collected on our servers.

8. Contact

In case of questions regarding data protection, please contact us at: karavanasistent@gmail.com
